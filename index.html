About my School

I went to high school at Academy High School in Plano ISD. It is a project based learning, STEM oriented school. Instead of testing out knowledge of subjects with repetitive practice and testing, we use the subject knowledge for practical applications, like using physics and geometry to design an accurate throwing machine, or rewriting Shakespeare preserving the themes and plot points in a setting based on current events for English and World Studies.
